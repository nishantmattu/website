About Me

I am currently a Software Engineer at Lockheed Martin. Previously, I have interned as a Software/Hardware Engineer Intern at Altera (Intel-PSG) and a Software Engineer Intern at Samsung. I am also obtaining a Master of Science in Computer Science online from Georgia Institute of Technology. I graduated from California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo in June 2016 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering.

Academic Experience

During my BSEE, I completed technical electives that covered foundational CS concepts, including object-oriented programming with Java, data structures, and design and analysis of algorithms. Through various EE classes, I also worked with low-level languages, such as C and VHDL, and embedded technologies.

Through my MSCS, I plan to take a wide array of courses that span across multiple facets of software engineering. Some of the courses I am interested in taking are machine learning, artificial intelligence, and operating systems. I have covered many of these topics on my own through online research, work experience, and personal projects but wanted to understand them from a more theoretical perspective as well.

Current Interests
